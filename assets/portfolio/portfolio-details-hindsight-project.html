Hindsight Project

About

This project was a group effort and definitely one of the most challenging ones we've done, especially since our professor was the one who suggested the topic. Our goal was to create an almanac or history-style website that highlights major events during the COVID-19 pandemic—stories that many of us weren’t able to catch up on in real time.

As the Web Designer and Associate Developer, I worked closely with my teammates to build and deliver a functional and meaningful output. We set out to produce a sample of how we could bring these stories together into one unified platform.We focused on six major topics that had the greatest impact during the pandemic: Education, Police Reports, Politics, Science and Technology (SCITECH), Sports, Transportation

In total, we hard-coded over 250 pages. While it wasn’t perfectly polished, we were proud of what we achieved. Our hard work paid off—we earned the highest grade in the batch and were exempted from the final exam for that subject. All the effort we put into creating the pages and telling the stories made it all worth it.

In a way, we felt that we were able to give justice not only to the website we created, but also to the stories that deserved to be told and remembered.
