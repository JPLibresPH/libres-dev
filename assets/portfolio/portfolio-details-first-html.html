My First HTML Page

About

I didn’t really think of this as a “project” back then, but it turned out to be very important for who I am today. Back in college, I wasn’t familiar with the basic structure of HTML — in fact, you could say I knew nothing about coding during my second year. I was only forced to do this because it was a requirement: a personal website project using only HTML. So I had to figure out how to make it work.

Thank God, I had a bit of knowledge in editing. I grabbed that opportunity and thought, "How can I apply my editing and design skills through coding?" And God heard me. The rest is history.

If it weren’t for this project, I probably wouldn’t have even considered becoming a Front-end Designer. It’s just satisfying to look back at it.
